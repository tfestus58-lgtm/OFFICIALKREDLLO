Common Questions

Buyers can pay using over 300 cryptocurrencies through NowPayments, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, USDC, BNB and many others. The exact project amount is displayed in USD and converted at the live rate at the time of payment. No bank account is required on either side.

Funds are released from escrow as soon as the buyer marks the project as complete or within 72 hours of delivery if the buyer takes no action. Pro plan freelancers receive an early payout option that skips the waiting period entirely. Once released, withdrawal to a crypto wallet processes automatically at the live exchange rate.

Either party can open a dispute while funds remain in escrow. Both sides submit evidence through the platform. A Kreddlo admin reviews all submissions and issues a fair ruling that splits or awards the escrow balance accordingly. Buyers who purchased Project Protection receive a senior reviewer and a guaranteed ruling within 48 hours.

KYC verification is processed entirely by Didit, a dedicated identity verification provider that operates under strict data protection regulations. Kreddlo does not store your identity documents. Only your verification status (verified or pending) is recorded in your Kreddlo account. All data on the platform is stored in Google Firebase with role-based access controls.
